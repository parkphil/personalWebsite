Projects

Interactive 3D Globe built with Cobe

This 3D globe was one of the first projects I built while teaching myself how to design on web with React. It was inspired by GitHub's 2022 homepage globe, which appeared on the site's landing page for five months.

Built with the cobe WebGL engine, the project displays a rotatable 3D globe on an HTML canvas and plots. Its interactive/customizable and automatically adjusts to different screen sizes!

The House America Built

The House America Built is an exhibition I helped organize at the Orange County Museum of Art alongside other student curators and Courtenay Finn, the museum's former chief curator. I focused on exhibition design and writing.

The exhibition considers how artists challenge structures of power that erase specific human stories, drawing from Michel Foucault's theory of power and knowledge.

This project was my first sustained encounter with exhibition-making, and it was very fun applying the art history skills I learned in high school. This will probably be my last serious engangement in curatorial studies, but I will always be a huge admirer of museums and art.
