Projects

Interactive 3D Globe

This 3D globe was one of the projects I built while teaching myself how to design on web with React. It was inspired by GitHub's 2022 homepage globe, which appeared on the site's landing page for roughly five months.

Built with the cobe WebGL engine, the project displays a rotatable 3D globe on an HTML canvas and plots location markers from geographic coordinates. It supports click-and-drag interaction and automatically adjusts to different screen sizes.

The House America Built

The House America Built is an exhibition I helped organize at the Orange County Museum of Art alongside Courtenay Finn, the museum's former chief curator. I focused on exhibition design and writing.

The exhibition considers how artists challenge structures of power that erase specific human stories in a postcolonial world, drawing from Michel Foucault's theory of power/knowledge.
